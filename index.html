HOW TO PLAY

Roll the dice each turn — swipe up on the tray, or hold the ROLL DICE button and release at the top of the power bar for a stronger roll.

Timing: release inside the magenta zone for a PERFECT roll — high numbers and a chance to force a double (crit).

Spend your roll on one of three abilities (some need a minimum roll), or stack the roll into your ultimate. When the ultimate bar fills, unleash a game-changing effect.

Elements: fire › elec › water › nature › fire. Hitting a weakness deals 1.5× damage.

Doubles = crit. Reduce the enemy's HP to zero to win.
